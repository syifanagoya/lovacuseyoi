What is Teallkh.Shop?

This online store claims to sell many of the products listed above. Before you decide to shop at this online store, there are many things you need to know.

Teallkh Shop has been found to be a scam site due to the following facts.

Contact Information:

The “CONTACT US”, and “TERMS of Service” pages provide information about its parent company, “Supic Company Limited”. Multiple scam and problematic websites have used it, including JacobcShop, Isoadi and Apdnisf. Grand-Kitchen and Tanisklep.

It has not provided its parent company’s name Supic Company Limited at the time of the review. However, it could change its address and parent company name in the future, as similar sites have changed their addresses and parent companies in the past.

Special Discounts and Sales Offers

Teallkh Shop lists many products at ridiculously low prices, which is almost impossible to find legitimate stores even on Cyber Monday or Black Friday.

Returns and Exchange

Its Return Policy does not allow for any type of exchange or return of products. This online store does not allow for returns or exchanges under any circumstances. These online stores have confusing returns and/or exchange provisions that make it almost impossible to get your full payment back.

Deliveries and Customer Complaints

According to complaints from buyers of similar online stores, the delivery times, customer support and after-sales services are very poor.

Our Final Verdict:

All the facts above indicate that Teallkh Shop may be a scam website.
