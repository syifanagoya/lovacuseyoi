Al-Nassr star Cristiano Ronaldo gifted custom-made watch worth staggering 92,000 featuring tradema

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Cristiano Ronaldo has been gifted a luxury watch worth £92,000 which features his trademark celebration on the back.

The timepiece has a garish green design and features 26 white diamonds as part of the custom design.

It also comes with black straps and a green lining, along with a signature and the words 'CR7' written in rose gold colours.

The 38-year-old is no stranger to high-value gifts and endorsements and looked delighted at the present from designers, Jacob & Co.

It comes as part of a new collection that is set to be released showcasing the best moments of Ronaldo's career.

This particular release comes with his trademark 'Siuu' celebration on the back.

The watch received by Ronaldo is called 'Heart of CR7 Baguette' and is one of four watches in the collection - with the most expensive priced at $145,000 (£115,000).

Announcing the collection on their Instagram page, Jacob and Co said: "Brand partner and friend Cristiano Ronaldo made an appearance at the Jacob & Co.'s Riyadh boutique inauguration on May 5th.

"The star striker of the Al Nassr football team was wearing the Jacob & Co. watch that bears his name and carries his image, the Jacob & Co x CR7 Epic X Heart of CR7."

Ronaldo is currently starring in Saudi Arabia for Al-Nassr after joining the club in January on a deal worth £177million-a-year.

Known for his love of watches, Ronaldo's collection is worth an estimated £5million.

And he also has a jewelry collection worth £3.4m and a fleet of supercars worth a staggering £18m.
