Deion Sanders' Daughter Slams Step Mom Pilar Sanders

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Deion Sanders‘ 19-year old daughter Deiondra Sanders isn’t buying her step mother’s story about the divorce. Yesterday Popular Critic told you that Pilar Sanders claimed she was blindsided by Deions’ divorce announcement. Her attorney Larry Friedman told TMZ Pilar was “heartbroken and surprised when [she] read … that Deion announced his decision, on his own, to end their marriage.”

It seemed a bit odd that just two months ago reports surfaced of a divorce but Pilar was still blindsided? Well Deion’s daughter had a few things to say about that….on Twitter no less.

“#HowYouDidntKnow but yo boxes have been packed for weeks now.”

Pilar also added that her top priority are her three children she shares with Deion.

“#HowYoTopPriorityBeen yo marriage and yo kids when YO OWN KIDS cant even talk to their brothers and sister without getting a whoopin?”

Yikes! Deiondra even accuses Pilar of playing victim and cheating!

“HowYoTopPriorityBeen yo marriage and yo kids when u flying out to see other n**gas … yeah we know.”

“All u had to do was be a supportive wife. Only thing u ever cared about doing with my dad is reality shows.”

“Pilar stop tryna play the victim. Maybe if u would of actually loved my dad and not loved the life he gave you we would be here today.”

It looks like this Deion Sanders’ divorce just took a nasty turn.
