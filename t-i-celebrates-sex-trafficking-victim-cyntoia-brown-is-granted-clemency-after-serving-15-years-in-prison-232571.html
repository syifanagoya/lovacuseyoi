T.I. Celebrates: Sex Trafficking Victim Cyntoia Brown Is Granted Clemency After Serving 15 Years In

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 20, 2024

A lot of rappers from the community, Kim Kardashian, and more prominent individuals have been supporting Cyntoia Brown.

The young woman had to serve 51 years in jail before she could become eligible for parole.

The Shade Room reported a while ago that ‘After news broke that Cyntoia Brown is required to serve 51 years before she’s eligible for parole, a Tennessee Governor named Bill Haslam is considering clemency for Cyntoia!’

Now, everyone is celebrating that fact that the young woman will finally be free. Kim Kardashian posted a message on her social media account and rapper T.I. did the same.
