Oliver Tree Life

Before he became a singer, Oliver made his name known to the world on YouTube in 2010. The title of his first solo record, “Splitting Branches,” and it came out on February 17, 2013. He got a recording deal with R&S Records in 2011 and learned music for a short time before his song “When I’m Down” became a hit in 2016. With his interview on “Last Call with Carson Daly” with Getter, Oliver made his first appearance on TV.

When his first Extended Play came out in 2018, it was called “Alien Boy.” His career reached new heights thanks to the shows he put on at big music events like Lollapalooza and Coachella. At this point in time, Oliver Tree has a deal with Atlantic Records. He is now worth a sum of $700,000 as of 2019.
