Who Is American Professional Golfer Danielle Kang Dating In 2021? Is She Married?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 26, 2024

Danielle Kang has slowly become a renowned golfer and at the age of 28 has achieved more than most do in the sport. The five-time international tour winner has had a stellar golfing career and plans to continue on her upward path. 

Kang is dating a fellow golfer and both of them have supported each other and guided each other in their careers. Both are decent golfers and are an even more adorable couple. Learn who is her golfer boyfriend and all about their relationship below. 

Who Is Danielle Kang Dating In 2021? 

Kang is in a relationship with American golfer Maverick McNealy. The two are a serious couple and have been together since at least 2019. 

They are still together in 2021 and attend each other’s tours and competitions as well. Everybody freaked out when they announced their relationship. 

The whole golfing world was shocked to learn about the power couple. In fact, the fans went berserk after learning about their romance. 

Kang and McNealy share an adorable relationship, as a matter of fact, Danielle revealed that they were an item and said that “He’s a dork. But he’s my dork.” when asked about her boyfriend. 

They made things public in 2019 but might have started dating sometime before that. As of 2021, they are enjoying a stable and happy relationship. There are many pro golfers that are dating other professional golfers, similarly, Kang and McNealy are also one of the power couples of golf in 2021.

Are Danielle Kang And Maverick McNealy Married? 

No, the power couple is not married as of July 2021, however, they are in a serious relationship and might want to get married in the near future. 

For the time being, the couple has chosen to keep things simple and not talk about any future wedding plans in the media. 

Danielle Kang Is A Golfing Superstar

Danielle Kang is 28 years old as of July 2021. She became a professional at the age of 18 in 2011. She quickly discovered that she had a talent for the pro tours as well. 

Slowly she started playing in international tournaments and has won 5 LPGAs. 

Her partner, Maverick is also a golfer and the heir to Scott McNealy. His father’s estimated net worth is around $800 million. While Maverick is a decent professional player he was once ranked as one of the top amateur golfers in the world.
