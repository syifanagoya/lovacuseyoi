Colin Egglesfield, Bio, Career, Married, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Colin Egglesfield is an American actor, widely recognized for his portrayal of Josh Madden in the long-running soap opera All My Children. He is also known for his appearance as Auggie Kirkpatrick on Melrose Place. 

Egglesfield came into the limelight when he appeared in the TNT T series titled Rizzoli & Isles. 

Colin was born on 9th February 1973, in Farmington Hills, Michigan. Born to his American parents William Egglesfield and Kathleen Egglesfield, he is of White ethnicity and holds an American nationality.

He spent most of his childhood in a Catholic family along with his siblings, a younger brother Sean Egglesfield, and a sister Kerry Egglesfield. At the age of ten, Colin along with his family moved to Morris, Illinois. There he studied at Marian Catholic High School and later attended the University of Lowa. 

The talented and handsome actor began his career by making a guest appearances in various television series including, Gilmore Girls, The Street, Nip/Tuck, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, etc.
